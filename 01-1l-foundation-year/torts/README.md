# Torts

## Why It Belongs In 1L

Torts is the cleanest entry into common-law reasoning. It teaches how courts reason from facts to standards, duties, liability, and defenses.

## Master These First

- intentional torts
- negligence
- duty
- breach
- actual cause
- proximate cause
- damages
- defenses
