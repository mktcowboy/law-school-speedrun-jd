# Constitutional Law

## Why It Belongs In 1L

Constitutional law gives structure to public power and rights analysis. Some schools place parts of this in `1L`, some later, but it belongs in the foundation sequence.

## Master These First

- judicial review as background architecture
- state action
- First Amendment basics
- equal protection
- due process
- levels of scrutiny

## Start Here

- National Archives Constitution transcript: <https://www.archives.gov/founding-docs/constitution>
