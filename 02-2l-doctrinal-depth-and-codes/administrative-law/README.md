# Administrative Law

## Why It Belongs In 2L

Administrative law is easier once you already know procedure, constitutional structure, and statutory reading.

## Master These First

- why agencies exist
- delegation and statutory authority
- rulemaking
- adjudication
- the APA at a high level
- judicial review

## Start Here

- Harvard-hosted H2O Administrative Law: <https://opencasebook.org/casebooks/8962-administrative-law/>
- Penn The Regulatory Review: <https://www.theregreview.org/>
- eCFR: <https://www.ecfr.gov/>
- Federal Register: <https://www.federalregister.gov/>
- GovInfo: <https://www.govinfo.gov/>

## Landmark Concepts

- notice-and-comment rulemaking
- arbitrary and capricious review
- `Chevron` as historical background
- `Skidmore`

## Best Supplement If You Need One

- only if administrative law becomes a core career subject

## Deliverables

- one `statute -> agency -> regulation -> review` map
- one APA terminology sheet
- one standards-of-review chart
