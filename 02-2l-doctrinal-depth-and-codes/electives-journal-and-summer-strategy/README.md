# Electives, Journal, And Summer Strategy

Back to the [subject index](../../SUBJECTS.md#core-2l-subjects).

## Why This Belongs In 2L

In a traditional JD path, `2L` is not just about doctrine. It is also where choices start to narrow future opportunities.

## What To Decide

- litigation-heavy versus transactional-heavy path
- whether journal work helps your goals
- whether clinic preparation should start now
- what kind of summer employer experience best fits your direction

## Good Elective Families

### If you lean litigation

- remedies
- federal courts
- trial advocacy
- advanced evidence

### If you lean business or transactional

- corporations depth
- securities regulation
- tax
- bankruptcy

### If you lean public law or government

- administrative law depth
- legislation and regulation
- state and local government
- criminal procedure depth

## Deliverables

- one `practice-interest memo` describing what kind of work you want to test
- one `course shortlist`
- one `summer-employer target list`

## Rule Of Thumb

Choose courses that make you more legible to the work you want, not just courses with the most prestigious-sounding titles.
