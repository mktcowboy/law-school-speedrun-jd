## One-Subject Mode

For a single-subject pass, use this order:

1. Open the subject guide.
2. Read its `Master These First` and `Start Here` sections.
3. Use the linked reading sequence for source order.
4. Build the deliverables in the [subject progress checklist](./templates/subject-progress-checklist-template.md).
5. Check major cases against the [landmark case dashboard](./00-start-here/assessment/landmark-case-dashboard.md) and [landmark case canon](./00-start-here/assessment/landmark-case-canon.md) when the subject is case-heavy.

These are the default foundation subjects. Do the [methods bridge](./00-start-here/methods/README.md) first if opinions, briefing, outlining, or short `IRAC` answers are new.

|---|---|---|---|---|

| Torts | early `1L` | fault, causation, injury, and common-law reasoning | build negligence, intentional-tort, strict-liability, and damages rule maps | [guide](./01-1l-foundation-year/torts/README.md), [1L sequence](./01-1l-foundation-year/01-reading-sequence-and-free-sources.md), [canon](./00-start-here/assessment/landmark-case-canon.md#torts) |
| Contracts | early `1L` | promise enforcement and private ordering | outline formation, interpretation, defenses, breach, and remedies | [guide](./01-1l-foundation-year/contracts/README.md), [1L sequence](./01-1l-foundation-year/01-reading-sequence-and-free-sources.md), [canon](./00-start-here/assessment/landmark-case-canon.md#contracts) |
| Property | middle `1L` | ownership, possession, land, and transfer systems | map possession, estates, transfers, servitudes, landlord-tenant, and takings | [guide](./01-1l-foundation-year/property/README.md), [1L sequence](./01-1l-foundation-year/01-reading-sequence-and-free-sources.md), [canon](./00-start-here/assessment/landmark-case-canon.md#property) |
| Criminal Law | middle `1L` | culpability, punishment, and defenses | outline actus reus, mens rea, homicide, inchoate crimes, and defenses | [guide](./01-1l-foundation-year/criminal-law/README.md), [1L sequence](./01-1l-foundation-year/01-reading-sequence-and-free-sources.md), [canon](./00-start-here/assessment/landmark-case-canon.md#criminal-law) |

These subjects make the curriculum more code-driven, rule-driven, and practice-facing.

| Subject | Best Time To Start | Use It For | First Good Goal | Links |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Evidence | after civil procedure and some torts/criminal law | admissibility, proof, trial sequence, and objections | map relevance, character, impeachment, hearsay, privileges, and expert proof | [guide](./02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/evidence/README.md), [2L sequence](./02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/01-reading-sequence-and-free-sources.md), [canon](./00-start-here/assessment/landmark-case-canon.md#evidence) |
| Criminal Procedure | after criminal law and constitutional structure | investigation, interrogation, prosecution, and exclusion | outline searches, seizures, confessions, identification, counsel, and exclusion | [guide](./02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/criminal-procedure/README.md), [2L sequence](./02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/01-reading-sequence-and-free-sources.md), [canon](./00-start-here/assessment/landmark-case-canon.md#criminal-procedure) |
| Business Associations | after contracts | entities, authority, governance, and fiduciary duties | map agency, partnerships, corporations, governance, and fiduciary liability | [guide](./02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/business-associations/README.md), [2L sequence](./02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/01-reading-sequence-and-free-sources.md), [canon](./00-start-here/assessment/landmark-case-canon.md#business-associations) |
| Commercial Law, UCC, And Secured Transactions | after contracts | sales, secured credit, and code reading | outline sales, negotiability basics, attachment, perfection, priority, and default | [guide](./02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/commercial-law-ucc-and-secured-transactions/README.md), [2L sequence](./02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/01-reading-sequence-and-free-sources.md), [UCC resources](./00-start-here/resources/open-resource-library.md#2l-reading-library) |
| Legislation And Regulation | after constitutional structure | statutory interpretation and regulatory context | map text, structure, purpose, canons, legislative history, and agency context | [guide](./02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/legislation-and-regulation/README.md), [2L sequence](./02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/01-reading-sequence-and-free-sources.md), [canon](./00-start-here/assessment/landmark-case-canon.md#legislation-and-statutory-interpretation) |
| Administrative Law | after legislation and constitutional structure | agency process, review, and remedies | outline rulemaking, adjudication, reviewability, standards of review, and remedies | [guide](./02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/administrative-law/README.md), [2L sequence](./02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/01-reading-sequence-and-free-sources.md), [canon](./00-start-here/assessment/landmark-case-canon.md#administrative-law) |
| Professional Responsibility | after you have some doctrine and writing practice | ethics rules and lawyer judgment | map duties to clients, courts, third parties, firms, and the profession | [guide](./02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/professional-responsibility/README.md), [2L sequence](./02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/01-reading-sequence-and-free-sources.md), [canon note](./00-start-here/assessment/landmark-case-canon.md#professional-responsibility) |
| Electives, Journal, And Summer Strategy | once core `2L` direction is visible | course and career planning | choose electives and writing work that support your practice direction | [guide](./02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/electives-journal-and-summer-strategy/README.md), [study pathways](./00-start-here/pathways/study-pathways.md), [3L tracks](./03-3l-practice-synthesis-and-launch/README.md) |

## Study By Goal

| Goal | Route | Use When |
|---|---|---|
| Traditional JD order | [1L](./01-1l-foundation-year/README.md) -> [2L](./02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/README.md) -> [3L](./03-3l-practice-synthesis-and-launch/README.md) | you want the most law-school-like progression |
| Bar-core pass | [bar-core pathway](./00-start-here/pathways/study-pathways.md#bar-core-pathway-nextgen-8) | you want the `NextGen` foundational subjects first |
| Litigation | [litigation pathway](./00-start-here/pathways/study-pathways.md#litigation-pathway) | you want disputes, motions, trial work, or appellate work |
| Transactional | [transactional pathway](./00-start-here/pathways/study-pathways.md#transactional-pathway) | you want entities, deals, drafting, or advisory work |
| Constitutional/public law | [constitutional law deep dive](./00-start-here/pathways/study-pathways.md#constitutional-law-deep-dive) | you want public law, clerkship-style reading, or administrative-state depth |
| Compressed schedule | [optional speedrun](./99-optional-speedrun/README.md) | you already know the subject goals and want a fixed sprint |
