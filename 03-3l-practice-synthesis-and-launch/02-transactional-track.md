# Transactional Track

Back to the [subject index](../SUBJECTS.md#3l-practice-and-launch).

One of two `3L` practice directions. Pick the track that matches the work you want; you can read both, but build deliverables in one. Pair this with the practice systems in this section.

## Who This Is For

Readers aiming at deal and advisory work — corporate, M&A, finance, commercial, startups and venture, and real estate.

## Core Subjects To Revisit

Reread these with a drafter's eye, focused on allocating risk rather than winning a fight:

- [business-associations](../02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/business-associations/README.md) — entity choice, authority, fiduciary duties, Delaware corporate law
- [commercial-law-ucc-and-secured-transactions](../02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/commercial-law-ucc-and-secured-transactions/README.md) — Article 2 sales and warranties, Article 9 secured transactions
- [legislation-and-regulation](../02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/legislation-and-regulation/README.md) and [administrative-law](../02-2l-doctrinal-depth-and-codes/administrative-law/README.md) — reading the statutes and regulations that govern deals
- [contracts](../01-1l-foundation-year/contracts/README.md) — drafting-grade command of conditions, representations, and remedies
- tax and bankruptcy as expansion subjects

## Drafting Exercises

- mark up a short agreement: identify representations, covenants, conditions, and indemnities
- draft a simple operating agreement or a bylaws skeleton
- draft a term sheet for a basic financing or sale
- draft a board resolution and a short closing checklist
- write a client advisory translating one regulation into action

## Research Emphasis

- entity, filing, and deal research
- statutes and codes, including the operative Delaware corporate code
- current governance and securities developments
- company-filings literacy

## Simulations

- run one mock deal: formation → governance documents → a financing or sale term sheet → closing checklist
- build a mock client file from [clinics-externships-and-practice-systems](./clinics-externships-and-practice-systems/README.md) for a small-business-formation matter
- push the writing through [advanced-writing-and-capstone](./advanced-writing-and-capstone/README.md)

## Hiring Relevance

- signals employers read: a transactional clinic, a deal-style writing sample (a marked-up agreement or a clear advisory memo), corporate and deal coursework
- journal matters less here than for litigation; precision and judgment in a drafting sample matter more
- a transactional writing sample should show risk-allocation thinking, not litigation rhetoric
