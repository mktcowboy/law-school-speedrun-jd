# Trial Advocacy And Motion Practice

Back to the [subject index](../../SUBJECTS.md#3l-practice-and-launch).

## Why It Belongs In 3L

This is where evidence, procedure, writing, and strategy finally converge in a realistic way.

## Focus Areas

- objections
- direct and cross structure
- opening and closing themes
- motion to dismiss and summary-judgment framing
- evidentiary arguments in context

## What To Build

- one mini trial notebook
- one motion outline
- one objection sheet
- one short oral-argument script

## Best Inputs

- your own evidence outline
- your civil procedure attack outline
- a small record or hypo you can reuse across motions and trial stages
