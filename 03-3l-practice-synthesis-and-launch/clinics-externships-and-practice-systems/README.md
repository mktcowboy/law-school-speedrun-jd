# Clinics, Externships, And Practice Systems

## Why It Belongs In 3L

By `3L`, you should stop studying law as only a library subject. You should simulate or enter actual practice systems.

## Focus Areas

- client intake
- file organization
- deadlines and procedure
- supervision
- professional judgment
- translating doctrine into work product

## What To Build In Self-Study Form

- one mock client file
- one document checklist
- one matter timeline
- one short reflection on judgment calls and unknowns

## Strong Practice Areas To Simulate

- housing or landlord-tenant
- small-business formation
- criminal defense intake and motion triage
- simple administrative benefit or licensing disputes
