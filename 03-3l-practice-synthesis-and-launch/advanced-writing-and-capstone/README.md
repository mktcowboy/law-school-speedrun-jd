# Advanced Writing And Capstone

## Why It Belongs In 3L

Final-stage legal training should produce writing that is cleaner, shorter, and more strategic than 1L memo work.

## Good Capstone Formats

- bench memo
- appellate-style brief
- regulatory comment
- client advisory
- comparative doctrine memo

## What To Build

- one substantial written product
- one revision log showing what improved
- one cross-subject capstone issue spotter

## Rule Of Thumb

The point is not just more pages. The point is better judgment about audience, authority, and structure.
